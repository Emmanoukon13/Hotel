Accomodation

Our rooms are designed for simplicity and comfort. We can accommodate from one to four persons, depending on the room. One can book single, standard double, sea-view double, and triple rooms. There is also the larger studio with kitchenette. And the economy rooms for the budget traveler.

All rooms at Hotel Paros are equipped with the following:

  • Air conditioning
  • Free Wi - Fi access
  • Private bath with shower
  • Hair dryer
  • Mini-bar
  • Flat screen tv
  • Heating

All standard rooms have private balconies.

The beach-side / sea view rooms have balconies with a direct sea- view. ( the early bird gets the worm!)

The budget / economy rooms do not have a balcony.

There is a daily cleaning service.

For any other special requests, please let us know before your arrival, in order for us to make arrangements for a comfortable and hospitable stay at Hotel Paros.

We will do our best to match and exceed your standards.
